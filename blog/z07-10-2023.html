PaW: A Paper a Week

Welcome to my new blog series: A Paper a Week!!! This ambitious series follows my attempts to find, read, and understand a published academic paper each week. Although there are no set topics or plans, being an undergrad CS student I plan to focus on CS related papers that are simple and easy enough to implement. The hope is to recreate the technology described in the paper in more or less a week, almost certainly more - much more - and hopefully have some cool projects to show off to other people, and learn interesting stuff along the way. Let's get started!
