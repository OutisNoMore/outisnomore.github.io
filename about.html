Who am I?

Hello World! My name is Jaemok "Christian" Lee. I am an aspiring programmer currently studying computer science at UC San Diego. My hobbies include watching films and dramas, and listening to music. Most of my experience comes from personal projects and classwork. In an effort to expand my horizons and interests, I have dabbled with creating web apps using node.js and a react framework, implementation of basic machine learning tools, and data structures and their related algorithms; all of the mentioned projects can be found on my github. Currently, as a part of UCSD's Early Research Scholars Program (ERSP), I am working in a team of 3 students to study the potential of repurposing old Android smartphones into carbon efficient sensors. I hope to make a career out of solving problems by writing organized and efficient programs with a purpose. I can be reached at my email: jal105@ucsd.edu
